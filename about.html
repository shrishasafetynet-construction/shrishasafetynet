OUR STORY

A decade of protecting Maharashtra's homes & sites

Shrisha Construction & Safety Nets started with a simple promise: deliver safety solutions the way we'd want them for our own homes — reliable, durable, and professionally installed. Over the years, thousands of successful installations have helped us become a trusted name across Maharashtra.

Today we serve residential societies, commercial buildings, construction sites, schools, factories, and sports facilities with premium safety nets, bird nets, turf solutions, and expert workmanship backed by quality assurance.
